FAQ

Common questions

How much of my time will this take?

Very little. One conversation about your business, plus whatever photos and info you'd like on the site. I handle everything else — design, building, domain, and launch.

Who owns the website when it's done?

You do — completely. The domain is registered in your name, and you own all the files and content. If we ever part ways, everything stays with you.

What about the domain and hosting costs?

Those are small ongoing costs paid directly by you: usually $10–20 per year for the domain, and hosting that's often free or a few dollars a month. I set both up and can manage them under the Care Plan.

How does payment work?

You get a fixed written quote before any work starts. Half is due up front, and the rest only when your site launches and you're happy with it.

What if I need changes after launch?

Small fixes are free for the first 30 days. After that, updates are covered by the Care Plan, or quoted per request — whichever works better for you.
