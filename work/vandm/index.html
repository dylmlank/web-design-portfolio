About Us

The team behind V&M Motor Care

V&M Motor Care is an Issaquah-based detailing service built on one simple idea: you shouldn't have to overpay for quality work. We detail cars, boats, and RVs with the same care and precision as the high-end shops — at a price that actually makes sense.

Whether it's a daily driver that needs freshening up or a weekend toy that deserves the full treatment, we bring the tools, the products, and the attention to detail to make your vehicle look its absolute best.

V&M Motor Care is run by two local high school students who are passionate about cars and that everyone should have a clean car.
